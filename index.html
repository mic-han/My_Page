IDEA: GreenMetrics, Crowd-Sourced Individual Sustainability Metrics Website

How do you know if you are sustainable? How do YOU compare?

  • Problem Statement

    It's difficult to know where you stand on sustainibility, both within your local community and worldwide. How does the number of plastic bags you use in a week compare to your neighborhood? What about someone in Australia? Thailand?

    View Details »
  • Solution

    That's where GreenMetrics comes in, an interactive website that allows users from all the world to self-report their sustainible metrics for greater global awareness.

    View Details »
  • Motivation

    Before stepping into a college dorm, I had little idea of how other people treated material use and their efficiency. By sharing ideas in this communal space, I was able to see where I stood on the sustainability scale and learned cool ways to be even more environmentally-friendly!

    View Details »
  • Sustainability Scale

    In GreenMetrics, a pop-out dashboard would highlight your standings in your local community, based on user data. Furthermore, there will be a self-reporting station as well as an interactive world map to see how everyone eles in the world is doing!

    View Details »
  • Why It Will Work

    People will engage with this website through social media movements, company partnerships, and cross-community competitions (such as a week-long UChicago versus Northwestern Student Sustainability Metrics Competition. Users are entered in drawings to win prizes!

    View Details »
  • Revenue Streams

    Advertising from sustainible companies, marketing analytics, and user data especially with general locations.

    View Details »
